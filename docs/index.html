Appendix 1. Automated Occupation Categorization with ChatGPT: Methodology Overview

To categorize occupations into fields of study, we utilized the ChatGPT powered by the OpenAI natural language model. For this study, we constructed the prompt used to interpret and classify 214 occupations (KidB 92 classification) into nine fields of study (DZHW Destatis-FÃ¤chersystematik), along with an Outside the area structure category. The natural language model within ChatGPT tracked the categorization process by analyzing the descriptions of occupations and assigning them to the most appropriate fields according to the given criteria. This automated approach simplified the categorization task, although it is not the sole method we are considering. We are now exploring and developing other approaches based on utilizing classifications from other datasets and developing classifications from real-world data.
